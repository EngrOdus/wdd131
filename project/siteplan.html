Site Purpose:

This site provide detail analysis on aluminum roof material calculations. The app ask you questions about your aluminum roof, calculate and return the total amount of meterials neede to complete your roof including the different types of materials needed to get your job done. This app is expected to be used by professionals.

Scenarios:

  • How many meters of aluminum roof materials will complete a two side pitched (gabled) roof rectangular shaped building of 24 meters long, 12 meters wide with roof kick-post of 4 meters high.
  • How many meters of aluminum roof materials will complete hipped roof of 4 sides with the building dimensions being 24 meters by 13 meters and roof kick-post measuring 5 meters high.
  • What is the total meters needed to complete a flat roof of 26 meters by 8.5 meters.
  • For more sophisticated roof designs, please provide the dimensions of the design/shapes of the roof.
