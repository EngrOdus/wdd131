Nigeria

Nigeria is the most populated country in africa with over 233.5 million people as of 2025 estimate. Nigeria is a home to 36 states and is located in west africa bordering Niger in the north, Chad by the north-eastern side, Benin Republic in the Eastern, and Cameroon in the east-southern side and stretches to the atlantic ocean in both the southern and western side. Nigeria is highly rich in cultural heritage with about 370 ethnic groups and speaks over 500 languages.
